About me

I'm an AI developer based in Córdoba, Argentina, currently completing my degree in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at IES21. I've spent the last two years building production-grade LLM applications — from RAG pipelines and multi-agent systems to full-stack web apps deployed on real infrastructure.

That experience taught me to think critically about what makes AI systems actually reliable — not just impressive in demos.
